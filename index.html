Installation Guide

ScoreDock is built by an independent developer. When you launch it for the first time, macOS may warn you that the app "is damaged and cannot be opened" due to strict internet download security.

To safely install and run it:

  1. Drag ScoreDock to your Applications folder.
  2. Open the Terminal app.
  3. Copy and paste the command below, then press Return:
xattr -cr /Applications/ScoreDock.app

Finally, launch ScoreDock from your Applications folder.

Requires macOS 14.0 or later. Optimized for Apple Silicon & Intel.
